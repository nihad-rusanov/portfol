About

Hello, I'm Nihat. I am a software engineer specializing in backend development. I have been working in this field for about 2 years and I am getting more and more excited every day. Backend development is a process that requires solving complex problems. I always have the opportunity to learn something new and update my current knowledge. It improves my creative thinking skills and increases my problem solving ability. Therefore, backend development has become a passion for me. Developing web applications, especially using Python and Django, is one of my favorite jobs. Django is a flexible and powerful web framework that makes developing web applications fast and easy. Also, developing using a programming language like Python makes my work much easier. The backend development business always offers the opportunity to learn and discover something new. Therefore, I try to constantly improve myself and follow new technologies. In this way, I can provide better service to my customers and make their work easier.
